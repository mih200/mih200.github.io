About Us

The Emerald Wyvern is a fantasy themed restaurant that takes inspiration from games like Dungeons & Dragons and Pathfinder in order to create a pleasant and unique atmosphere for its clients.

Offers & Services

Party Discounts

Bring your friends and you can benefit from a 10% discount at the end of your meal. Hey, everyone, get in here!

Enchanting Music

Enjoy a pleasant atmosphere by listening to a music meant to heal one's mind, body and soul. Beware of possible wyvern sounds.

Delivery by Wyvern

You can't reach The Emerald Wyvern? No worries, our wyvern riders stand ready to deliver your meals on almost any Plane of Existence!

Varva the Emerald Claw

Those that visit us have a chance to take a picture of (and maybe even pet) Varva, the Emerald Claw, our resident Wyvern.
