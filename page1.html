Specials

The best meals for brave adventurers
Beginner's Trial
27 RON - 400 g

A tasty soup, with meat, spaghetti, potatoes and a few other vegetables. Perfect for someone that is preparing to start a new adventure.

Slayer's Gamble
38 RON - 480 g

A dish made out of meat, cheese, vegetables and a bit of sauce (with some extra bread). Good for a seasoned adventurer, that if getting ready for a fight.

Varva's Hoard
77 RON - 1 kg

Only for the most heroic adventurers. This meal contains everything needed to take on a wyvern. It is recommended that you share it with a friend.

Appetizers

Outlander's Meal

24 RON - 380 g

For the group's frontliners. Wild boar meat (actually your average pork) with magical herbs (vegetables), wrapped in dough.

Ranger's Breakfast

15 RON - 290 g

Quick to make, easy to eat and not too heavy for your stomach. A good meal if you want to start your day early and as soon as possible.

Elf's Secret Salad

18 RON - 320 g

A secret salad recipe, only known by the elves, until a rogue stole it. Nowadays, you find it at every restaurant with a fantasy theme.

Main Dish

Wanderer's Quest

31 RON - 440 g

Legend says that a long time ago, someone asked a group of adventurers to recover a food recipe from the ruins of a forgotten kingdom. Halfway through the journey, the bard made a forgery and deceived the quest giver into thinking that it was the real deal.

Wizard's Choice

35 RON - 450 g

A dish that was at first conjured up by a wizard that wanted to taste his mother's cooking one last time before venturing into another Plane of Existance.

Basilisk Slices

37 RON - 460 g

Indeed, a mighty basilisk was slain to prepare this meal. Most adventurers were surprised to find out that it tastes like chicken.

Paladin's Respite

19 RON - 240 g

A somewhat traditional dessert among paladins. Usually eaten after vanquishing a great evil.

Druid's Cake

20 RON - 250 g

A cake made out of ingredients found by the druids that wander through the woods.

Drinks

The Mimic

16 RON - 400 ml

A drink that is not what it seems. Many adventurers have fallen in traps laid by mimics and met their end. This one is harmless and also tasty.

The Doppelganger

18 RON - 400 ml

It shifts its color like a doppelganger that is taking on a new disguise. A drink that is absolutely hated by their kind. Challenge one of your party members to drink one if you suspect that they have been replaced by such a creature.

Fey Surprise

20 RON - 400 ml

We guarantee that the fey had no ill intentions when they made the recipe for this drink. Don't ask us what we had to give in exchange for learning how to make it.
